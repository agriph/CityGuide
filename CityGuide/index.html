A cityguide with focus on Reykjavik.

Welcome to a new cityguide about Reykjavik, the capital of the land of ice and fire. Here you'll be able to find useful information about various things that you can do, see and eat in this historical town of less than 130 000 residents. Interested in history? - You're in the right place. Head over to "Historical Places" to find out more. Relatives in Iceland? - Look no further; you'll find som on that too, as well. We also have some advice to those who are looking to learn icelandic or for the adventurers who plan on moving to Iceland. For questions or inquries please go to "Contact & Other".
